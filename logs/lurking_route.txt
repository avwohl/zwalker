The Lurking Horror — verified route, milestone by milestone
(point values from the ZIL source; 19 x 5 = 95 earned incrementally, the
kill then sets SCORE=100 outright, absorbing the unreachable curtain 5.
See logs/lurking_notes.md for file:line citations.)

 1. Terminal Room: sit, turn on pc, login 872325412 / password uhlersoth
    (feelie copy protection), click menu, click paper, read paper, wait
    through the three remaining pages of the corrupted text, faint.
 2. Yuggoth dream: down, down to the platform, TAKE STONE  [+5 = 5],
    wait 3 turns; the lurker seizes you and you wake at your terminal.
    The stone stays with you.
 3. Wait while the hacker fixes your files (he mentions "Lovecraft" and
    the Department of Alchemy), then ASK HACKER ABOUT KEYS so he shows
    off the master key.
 4. Kitchen (s, w): open fridge, take coke and carton, microwave the
    Chinese food 5:00 on HI (heat 20 = hot but not ruined), take carton.
 5. Terminal Room: GIVE CARTON TO HACKER, then HACKER, GIVE ME MASTER KEY
    [+5 = 10]. Drop the now-useless assignment.
 6. Elevator down (press down, wait for the doors, in): OPEN PANEL, TAKE
    FLASHLIGHT, press 1, out at the Computer Center lobby, stairs down.
 7. Temporary Basement (e): take gloves and crowbar, WEAR GLOVES.
 8. Aero Basement (w, w): board forklift, start it, drive e/e/e into Dead
    Storage, flashlight on, MOVE JUNK WITH FORKLIFT x4, dismount, e into
    Ancient Storage [+5 = 15].
 9. OPEN MANHOLE WITH CROWBAR, down [+5 manhole = 20], n to the Renovated
    Cave, down to the altar, TAKE KNIFE, back up/s/up, west out through
    Dead Storage (flashlight off in the lit basement).
10. Infinite Corridor west end (w, w, w to stairs, up, s): TAKE CONTAINER
    (Frobozz floor wax). Follow the waxer east (it moves every 5 turns;
    you cannot pass its room). At the emergency cabinet (INF-4): SMASH
    CABINET WITH CROWBAR, TAKE AXE, CUT CORD WITH AXE, OPEN CONTAINER,
    POUR WAX ON FLOOR — the maintenance man descends, charges, and slips
    in the wax [+5 = 25]. Drop the empty container and leave him
    slipping (he dissolves into squealing creatures a few turns later).
11. Great Dome (w to INF-3, up): CLIMB ROPE wearing the gloves — it is a
    tentacle; it dumps you on the catwalk and flees [+5 Top of Dome = 30].
    Open the metal door, out into the blizzard (freeze clock ~22 turns),
    up to the tip, TAKE PLUG, DROP PLUG, TAKE PAPER — the suicide note
    [+5 = 35]. Back inside, LOWER LADDER, down, down.
12. Brown Building (e, e, n, d, se): TAKE BOOTS, WEAR BOOTS, up to the
    lobby, up the long stairs, UNLOCK DOOR WITH KEY, open, w onto the
    skyscraper roof, up into the weather dome. The flier smashes against
    the dome. DIG IN DIRT (the peach-tree tub), TAKE HAND [+5 = 40],
    down to the roof — the flier is waiting — THROW STONE AT CREATURE:
    both go over the parapet, the flier chasing the stone [+5 = 45].
    Inside, down to the lobby, OUT to the Small Courtyard, TAKE STONE,
    back in.
13. Alchemy (d, nw, u, s to INF-5, s, knock on door, wait): the professor
    ushers you in. SHOW NOTE TO PROFESSOR (his student's suicide note —
    his manner turns predatory), s into the lab: he chalks you into the
    pentagram as the sacrifice. Wait 2 turns (cutting earlier gets your
    inventory confiscated), CUT LINE WITH KNIFE, EXIT PENTAGRAM, MOVE
    BENCH, OPEN TRAPDOOR, DOWN — the summoned thing takes the professor
    instead [+5 = 50]. Flashlight on (the tunnel is pitch dark and
    darkness kills).
14. Back up through the trapdoor: the lab is wrecked, the vat intact.
    PUT HAND IN VAT (Elixir of Life), wait 2 turns, TAKE HAND — animated,
    it perches on your shoulder [+5 = 55]. Drink the Coke here (pushes
    the turn-200 exhaustion clock past the end of the game).
15. Walk the corridor west; the urchin (random wanderer, seed-pinned)
    turns up — SHOW HAND TO URCHIN: he flees screaming and drops the
    BOLT CUTTER; take it [+5 = 60].
16. Stash axe + bolt cutter + flask (fetched from the Temporary Lab,
    flashlight on inside) + knife + coke at the Basement elevator: the
    Tomb crack rejects anything bigger than the crowbar.
17. Tomb (w, w, d, nw): UNLOCK PADLOCK WITH KEY, TAKE PADLOCK, OPEN
    HATCH, flashlight on, down into the muggy Steam Tunnel [+5 = 65].
18. Rats: e to the valve, OPEN VALVE WITH CROWBAR (loosens it), wait 3
    turns while the rat horde arrives and attacks, OPEN VALVE WITH
    CROWBAR again — live steam scalds the troop [+5 = 70]. CLOSE VALVE,
    e, e to the wall triad, MOVE BRICK WITH CROWBAR, MOVE NEW BRICK WITH
    CROWBAR — a hole with a steel reinforcing rod. Return up the hatch.
19. Elevator pit: at the Basement doors OPEN DOORS WITH CROWBAR, PUT
    CROWBAR IN DOORS (wedge), s down into the Concrete Box [+5 = 75].
    TAKE CHAIN, PUT CHAIN AROUND ROD, LOCK CHAIN WITH PADLOCK, climb up,
    PUT CHAIN ON HOOK (the machinery under the parked car), TAKE CROWBAR.
20. Ride: upstairs, press up, in, PRESS 2 — the car climbs, the chain
    snaps taut and rips the rod and half the wall out from below
    [+5 = 80]. Out at 2, stairs back down to the Basement.
21. Final descent (one-way): take axe/cutter/flask, wedge the doors
    again, flashlight on, into the pit, N through the ripped wall, w x5
    along the steam tunnel, w into the Muddy Tunnel, d to the Large
    Chamber of urchin slots, CUT WIRE WITH CUTTER — the pseudo-urchins
    collapse [+5 = 85].
22. Wet-tunnel maze (fixed layout): d, n, d, s, s, d to the slime
    curtain. OPEN FLASK, POUR LIQUID ON CURTAIN — liquid nitrogen
    freezes and shatters it, exposing an ancient door [+5 = 90].
    UNLOCK DOOR WITH KEY, open, s — it locks behind you.
23. Inner Lair (hacker death-countdown running): the hand dives into the
    pool. OPEN COVER, UNPLUG COAXIAL CABLE, SEARCH POOL (finds the
    high-voltage line), TAKE LINE, CUT LINE WITH AXE x3 (the hacker
    walks in and is absorbed by the mass meanwhile), TAKE LINE, PUT LINE
    IN SOCKET — sparks; the mass compacts into the frob [+5 = 95].
24. Wait one turn (it tenses; the stone glows red-hot), THROW STONE AT
    CREATURE — it swallows itself [SCORE set to 100]. TAKE STONE: it
    cracks open, something small flies off into the dark, the hacker
    crawls out of the mud ("Can I have my key back?") — game over,
    100/100, President of the Institute.
