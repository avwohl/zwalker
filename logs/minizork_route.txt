MINI-ZORK I — verified route (in my own words)
================================================
Binary minizork.z3 (R34/S871124), max 350. Seed pinned after start() (boot is
RNG-free). The win needs SCORE to be EXACTLY 325 as the 15th treasure enters
the trophy case (fires +25 -> 350, reveals the map, opens the SW Barrow exit);
every scored action below happens exactly once and the run never dies.

Point sources (total 350):
  Treasures, points on first TAKE (215): egg 6, painting 7, torch 12,
    coffin 13, sceptre 9, skull 22, trunk 15, jade 13, bracelet 10, diamond 25,
    coins 11, chalice 20, emerald 18, scarab 15, pot of gold 19.
  Room entries (55): Kitchen 10, Cellar 25, Thief's Lair 20.
  Events (55): kill troll 10, open dam/drain reservoir 20, pray at altar 15,
    reach Drafty Room lit 10.
  Completion bonus: 15th treasure cased at 325 -> +25 = 350.

The house Living Room (trophy case) is the hub. The trap door bars behind you
(one-way down); the return is Cyclops-Room -> east -> Living Room, opened for
good once you say ULYSSES. The Living Room is SACREDBIT (thief-free), used as a
tool stash. The ivory torch is the main light (lamp lasts only ~140 turns);
lamp is backup + the coal-mine light.

TRIP 1 — house, egg, troll, torch, coins, cyclops
  Grab the jeweled egg up the forest tree; enter the Kitchen window (+10);
  take lamp + sword (Living Room), open the case, move rug, open trap door;
  fetch rope + knife from the Attic; case the egg. Down to the Cellar (+25),
  east to the Studio for the painting, back and north to the Troll Room; kill
  the troll with the sword (+10) and HEAL to full (wounds cut carry capacity).
  Via the Round Room take the Dome rope down to the Temple for the torch (drop
  the sword, switch off the lamp), out through the Altar/Windy Cave, and through
  the maze (w,nw,d to the skeleton) for the bag of coins. Say ULYSSES at the
  Cyclops Room (opens the Living-Room shortcut), return and case painting+coins.
  [running score ~81; the thief usually snatches the painting en route -> it is
  recovered from his lair in Trip 6, still counted since take-value fired once.]

TRIP 2 — dam drain + trunk
  Down to the Dam: take the matchbook; in the Maintenance Room press the yellow
  button and take the wrench + screwdriver; turn the bolt with the wrench to
  open the sluice gates (+20, reservoir drains). Cross the drained reservoir for
  the trunk of jewels (+15). Return through the maze, case the trunk, and stash
  the matches + screwdriver on the Living-Room floor.  [~116]

TRIP 3 — crystal skull (exorcism)
  Carry the matches down; take the brass bell (Temple) and the black book +
  candles (Altar). At the Entrance to Hades perform the ceremony: ring bell,
  re-take candles, light match, light candles with match, read book (the gate
  spirits flee). Enter Hades for the skull (+22), drop the ceremony items,
  return and case the skull.  [~138]

TRIP 4a — sceptre + rainbow pot of gold
  Down to the Egyptian Room, open the coffin, take just the sceptre; at the
  Altar PRAY (+15) — this teleports you (sceptre in hand) up to Forest Edge.
  Cross to the End of the Rainbow, wave the sceptre (the rainbow solidifies and
  the pot of gold appears), take the pot (+19). Walk back over the surface
  (thief-free) and case sceptre + pot.  [~181]

TRIP 4b — the gold coffin
  Down again, take the size-55 coffin (too heavy to carry with anything much,
  and it can only leave the Altar by PRAY), pray up to Forest Edge, walk home
  and case the coffin (+13).  [~194]

TRIP 5 — Frigid River: emerald + scarab
  Fetch the air pump from Reservoir North; at the Dam Base inflate the boat,
  launch, and let the current drift you to River-3; grab the red buoy, land east
  on Sandy Beach, open the buoy for the large emerald (+18). Dig the Sandy Cave
  sand four times for the jeweled scarab (+15) — a 5th dig collapses the hole
  (death). Exit over the now-solid rainbow / canyon / forest to the house; case
  emerald + scarab.  [~227]  (river rooms are SACREDBIT, so the thief can't
  touch you here.)

TRIP 6 — kill the thief (recover loot), keep the torch
  Through the maze to the Cyclops Room, up to the Thief's Lair (+20). Kill the
  thief with the knife (safe at this score, to-hit ~72%); his death drops the
  silver chalice plus everything he stole (the painting, etc.) in the lair.
  HEAL, take it all, drop the stiletto, return and case the chalice (+20) and
  the recovered painting.  KEEP the torch — the mine needs it, and the thief is
  now dead so it can't be stolen.  [~267]

TRIP 7 — coal mine: jade, bracelet, diamond
  Take the stashed screwdriver + the Kitchen garlic (or the bat flies you off).
  Down to the Bat Room for the jade figurine (+13). At the Shaft Room stow the
  torch + screwdriver in the basket. On the LAMP only (a lit flame in the Gas
  Room = death) cross the Gas Room for the sapphire bracelet (+10) and fetch the
  coal from the Ladder Room; back at the Shaft Room add the coal to the basket
  and lower it. Return, drop everything at the Ladder Room (the squeeze west
  needs empty hands), enter the torch-lit Drafty Room (+10), take torch/coal/
  screwdriver from the basket, and in the Machine Room open the lid, put coal
  in, close, turn the switch with the screwdriver -> huge diamond (+25). Basket
  the diamond/torch/screwdriver back up (raise chain), reclaim your gear, exit
  via the Bat Room and the Slide-Room chute to the Cellar, and through the maze
  to the Living Room. Case jade, bracelet, diamond and finally the torch.
  The 15th treasure at exactly 325 -> +25 = 350, "Look to your treasures for
  the final secret."

ENDGAME — Stone Barrow
  Take the newly-revealed parchment map from the case, read it (points SW to
  the Stone Barrow), walk out to West of House (the secret SW path is now open),
  and go SW into the Barrow: "You have completed a great and perilous adventure
  ... You have mastered the first part of the ZORK trilogy."  = WIN, 350/350.
