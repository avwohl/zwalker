Pentari — verified route to the maximum 70/70 ending, in my own words.
(Fixed 35-command list; verified by replay_solve at seed 3. Commands in CAPS.)

Getting into the castle
  N, N              Quarters -> Compound -> Barracks (the Pentarian Transporter).
  IN                Step into the transporter.
  CITY              Cast the transporter word. It misfires to the Wizard's Guild
                    Entrance Hall instead of the Bank.  (+5 -> 5)
  E                 Into the Library; the wizard Morden is here.
  COVERT            Cast Morden's "word of bringing". You are teleported into
                    Vamvevmew's castle (the Castle intersection) and made covert,
                    so the Dark Elf wanders "looking for" you but can't attack.

Break the ward and gather gear
  S                 Castle -> Castle Entrance, where the Magical Seal (Morden's
                    anti-magic ward) sits.
  SMASH SEAL        Shatter the ward so your magic (Fwoosh) will work.  (+10 -> 15)
  N, N, N           Entrance -> Castle -> Main Hall -> Main Hall by the Fireplace.
  IN                Climb into the Large Fireplace; a dirty scroll is here.
  GET SCROLL        The scroll teaches the Fwoosh (fireball) spell.  (+10 -> 25)
  OUT               Back to Main Hall by the Fireplace.
  S                 -> Main Hall.
  E                 -> Spiral Stairway.
  N                 -> Dining Room; a folded linen towel is on the floor.
  GET TOWEL         (Used to wrap/carry the emerald; not itself scored.)
  S                 -> Spiral Stairway.
  W                 -> Main Hall.
  W                 -> Armory; Galin's jewel-encrusted dagger is here.
  GET DAGGER        Take the dagger.  (+5 -> 30)
  N                 -> Treasury; the damaged, locked oaken chest is here.

Free the emerald (must be done with the elf NOT in the Treasury)
  OPEN TOWEL        Unfold the towel.
  FWOOSH            Summon a fireball; it blasts the oaken chest open. (If the
                    elf were in the room here it would hit HIM and get you
                    killed — the recorder waits for him to leave first.) (+10->40)
  LOOK              (Settles the room view.)
  GET SMALL EMERALD Take the freed "daughter" emerald. This shatters the whole
                    anti-magic zone; Morden senses it and teleports in to duel
                    Vamvevmew somewhere in the castle.  (+5 -> 45)

Meet the elf, kill him, summon Galin
  S, E              Treasury -> Armory -> Main Hall (the four-exit hub the
                    wandering elf passes through most often).
  Z, Z              Wait. Carrying the emerald, your "aura of power" gives you
                    away; the elf enters, cracks you on the head, steals the
                    emerald, and yanks you into the Upper Level where Morden and
                    Vamvevmew are locked in sorceral combat.
  KILL ELF          While the elf is distracted by Morden you come up behind him
                    and run him through with your sword; he dies.  (+10 -> 55)
  GET SMALL EMERALD Reclaim the emerald the elf dropped.
  PUT SMALL EMERALD ON BOX   Crown the platinum box in the summoning circle.
  LOOK              The box glows; Duke Galin materializes, finds his enemy's
                    corpse ("Damn it, I wanted the privilege."), and rewards you.
                    *** You have won ***  70 out of a possible 70.  (+15 -> 70)

Notes
- The IF Archive walkthrough stops at 60/70 by skipping the duel: it hurries the
  emerald upstairs and crowns the box alone, so Galin kills the elf offstage.
  Meeting the elf and killing him yourself is what adds the last 10 points.
- The only randomness is the elf's wander; the adaptive recorder
  (scripts/solve_pentari_adaptive.py) handles the FWOOSH timing and the hub wait
  and records every command, so the fixed list above replays deterministically.
