Deephome — route in my own words (verified 300/300, seed 1)

Legend: capitalised = a milestone / score change.

OPENING
- Secret Entrance: the barred door has three carved symbols (tree=elves,
  house=humans, mountain=dwarves). PUSH MOUNTAIN -> the door clicks open (+5).
  sw into the city.
- Northern Guard Post: open the cabinet, take + WIELD the Sword, take + WEAR the
  shield (needed for the Eranti fight much later). sw.
- Main Hall: the dark spirit Indanaz paralyses you. Wait it out (five z's), then
  leave. Go straight s to N. Main Street — deliberately NOT up to Dwarven
  Village, because skipping that one optional room keeps the final score at the
  clean 300 instead of the off-by-one 301.

STREET / TEMPLE / ROYAL SWEEP (collecting location points + the moss)
- N. Main Street -> Clothier (ne) -> back (sw) -> Bakery (e) -> back (w) ->
  Scrivener's (w): open the desks so the blank paper is takeable later.
- e, s to S. Main Street; e into the Dwarven Library (just for the point — no
  book lookups are needed); w back, then w into the Great Temple of Kraxis
  (Indanaz has moved here). e, s to the Intersection: drop the King's Order.
- w to East Royal Road; s to Lesser Noble's Palace; n, w to West Royal Road:
  TAKE MOSS (glowing green, repels the terrock). n to Greater Noble's Palace;
  s, w to Outer Court; w to the Throne Room (the benign spirit Cholok is here).
- Out east five times to Eastern Guard Post; se to City Gates: OPEN the panel
  HATCH now (the gear goes here later). nw, w, s to Extreme South Main Street.

POWER
- sw to Water Works (the wheel does nothing yet). ne, s to Mining Center; s to
  Ore Mines: TAKE the pickaxe ("axe"). n, e to Coal Mines: DIG ROCK WITH AXE ->
  a lump of coal; TAKE COAL (+10). w, n, e to the City Generator: open furnace,
  PUT COAL IN FURNACE, close furnace, PULL LEVER -> POWER ON (+30).

RAIL LOOP 1 — gear, then scouting the wings
- Back to the Main Hall railway (w,n,n,e,w,n,n,w). The rail car travels between
  four wings; board with "enter car", travel with "push <colour> button", leave
  with "out".
- yellow -> Smithy Court (nw). sw to the Blacksmith: open the forge, TAKE the
  GEAR. Wander the smithy court for its points (Silversmith, Carpenter's, Common
  Forge, Slag Pit, Goldsmith).
- Smithy railway, green -> Barracks (w). Sweep Soldiers' Barracks, Armory, Mess
  Hall, Training Grounds, and the Waterfall (scout: the terrock nests in the
  intake pipe). Back to the Barracks railway.
- red -> Treasury (sw). Treasury Antechamber; n to the Common Bank (deposit box
  is locked); w to the Common Vault. Back to the Treasury railway.

WATER, then the GATE
- yellow -> Main Hall; immediately green -> Barracks; w, sw, sw to the Waterfall:
  PUT MOSS IN NEST -> the terrock leaves (+10); PUSH ROD to swing the intake
  pipe clear.
- ne, ne, e; yellow -> Main Hall; e, s, s, s, s, sw to Water Works: TURN WHEEL ->
  WATER ON (+30).
- ne, n, e, se to City Gates: PUT GEAR ON POST, close hatch, PUSH LEVER (set to
  "Gate"), PUSH BUTTON -> the GATE OPENS (+40).

THE WOODS AND EMBER — clover + the Eranti
- s to Deephome Entrance; w, w to Great Woods Limits. The enchanted woods path
  is w, sw, w, s, e, n, w, e -> Forest Clearing: X CLOVER (reveals it), then TAKE
  FOUR LEAF CLOVER (for the temple rite). Leave s, e; then e, e, s, s to the
  Wooded Path (note the squirrel + acorns — trapped later), s to Town of Ember,
  w to Shops, e, s to Ember's Town Square, w to the Ember Tool Shop.
- Ask the young man about the hammer (he'll give it once the Eranti is gone) and
  about the eranti. e, s to the Pond: KILL ERANTI repeatedly until it dies (+20)
  — the sword+shield keep the dwarf healing safely (10 blows at seed 1). n, w
  back to the shop; ask about the hammer again, TAKE HAMMER.

FORGE THE PICK
- Long walk back to the Main Hall (e, then n×5, nw, w, n×3): TAKE the magical
  TORCH (a fire source). w; yellow -> Smithy; nw, sw to the Blacksmith:
  take scrap, PUT SCRAP IN FURNACE, BURN COAL WITH TORCH, z (scraps glow),
  TAKE SCRAPS (they merge), PUT SCRAPS ON ANVIL, HAMMER SCRAPS -> a sharp
  ("metal") pick; TAKE METAL PICK.

KEY, COIN
- ne, se; red -> Treasury; sw, n to the Common Bank: UNLOCK BOX WITH METAL PICK,
  open box, LOOK IN BOX (reveals it), TAKE LARGE KEY. s to the Antechamber:
  UNLOCK DOOR WITH LARGE KEY, drop the key, open door, w into The Treasury
  (the shadow spirit Kebarn is here): SEARCH WEALTH (reveals it), TAKE GOLD COIN
  (+10). e, ne; yellow -> Main Hall.

NET, SQUIRREL, HOST
- e, s, s to S. Main Street; the shack path is s, e, se, s, s, s, s, s, e ->
  Small Shack: UNLOCK CHEST WITH METAL PICK, open chest, TAKE NET. w, n, n to
  the Wooded Path: DIG GROUND WITH AXE, PUT NET ON HOLE, TAKE ACORNS, PUT ACORNS
  IN NET, CLIMB TREE -> the squirrel springs the net (+10); down, TAKE NET.
- To the Throne Room (n, n, n, nw, w, w, w, w, w): OPEN NET -> the benign spirit
  Cholok inhabits the squirrel and leaves (+20). The net is now free again.

BANISH INDANAZ (temple)
- e, e, e, e to the Intersection: drop the axe and the metal pick (no longer
  needed). n, n, w to Scrivener's: TAKE PAPER. e, e to the Bakery: TAKE BOTTLE,
  FILL BOTTLE (water is on now), BURN BLANK PAPER WITH TORCH -> ashes. w, s, w
  to the Great Temple: DROP COIN, POUR WATER ON COIN, PUT ASHES ON COIN, PUT
  CLOVER ON COIN, PRAY TO KRAXIS, MANAZ -> Indanaz is banished (+30). TAKE COIN,
  TAKE ALL FROM COIN (recover the clover etc.).

BANISH KEBARN (treasury) -> WIN
- e, n, e to the Bakery: FILL BOTTLE again. w, n, w to the Main Hall railway;
  red -> Treasury; sw, w into The Treasury: OPEN NET, DROP NET, PUT COIN IN NET,
  POUR WATER ON NET, TAKE COIN, PRAY TO KRAXIS, MANAZ -> Kebarn is banished
  (+40). With power, water, gate, and all three spirits done:
  *** You have won ***  —  "300 out of a possible 300 ... remembered forever."
