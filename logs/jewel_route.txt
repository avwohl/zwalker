The Jewel of Knowledge — route in my own words (Release 2, seed 1, 90/90)

Legend: >>> marks a scoring action (running cumulative total in brackets).

0. INTRO SKIP
   bypass -> yes. Lands on the Rocky Plateau (Sixth Layer) with just the sword.

1. ROCKY PLATEAU / MINERALIZED CORRIDOR / TIGHT FIT
   Take Jacob's travelling sack (it holds moon salt). verbose. Go w, nw into the
   Mineralized Corridor.
   >>> pull quartz  -> quartz shatters, revealing a fault line          [2]
   Go u to the Tight Fit; you can't climb up carrying everything, so d, drop all,
   then u, u.
   >>> the second u reaches Fifth Layer Dropoff (a "visit" point)       [4]

2. REFIND FIREBUG + BOOK
   The firebug sits on an outcrop over a gaping hole; you can't reach it, and
   throwing the book AT the bug would kill it. So:
   >>> throw book at outcrop  -> outcrop cracks; book AND bug fall below[7]
   d, d back to the Mineralized Corridor; take all; se, e to Rocky Plateau; take
   bug, take book.

3. VISIT THE DRUIDS
   open sack; rub bug (lights it); light salt with bug;
   >>> smell smoke  -> transported to the Amylyan Druids' Temple        [9]
   Talk to Allarah: tell allarah about jacob; ask about jewel, helspeth, white
   dragon, then:
   >>> ask allarah about black dragon  -> she gives the ebony key       [12]
   >>> enter mist  -> returns you to the Rocky Plateau                  [14]

4. MOSS, TRAPDOOR, LYE, SKELETON  (west chain)
   w, w, w to Carved Steps; n to the Moss-Filled Cave. take moss.
   >>> clean dirt with moss  -> uncovers a wooden trapdoor             [16]
   open trapdoor; d to the Cleaning Closet; take lye; u, s, s to the Lonely
   Burial Ground. pour lye on moss (makes it foamy):
   >>> clean skeleton with moss  -> skeleton collapses, revealing the
       air bladder and crampons                                        [19]
   take bladder; read label; take crampons.

5. GEYSER CLIMB TO ARIANA
   n, e, e, nw, w, w to the Gaseous Geyser (erupts on the 4th turn after entry).
   Examine hole/shaft/vapor to burn turns, wear crampons, then:
   >>> climb porous  -> climb the porous wall to Shaft Base (a visit pt)[21]
   u, u, e to On the Ledge (Ariana's body). look in boot:
   >>> take lockpick  (a sundry item point)                            [23]
   Also take bow (the crossbow, arrow inside).

6. BLOW THE POROUS WALL
   w, d, d, d back to the Gaseous Geyser (erupts again on the 4th turn).
   x porous, search porous, break porous (too weak), then:
   >>> put bladder in hole  -> the blocked geyser bursts the porous
       wall open, opening the Quarry                                   [26]
   look; take bladder; w to the Quarry; take pickaxe.

7. CROSSBOW PRACTICE
   e, e, e, se, w, w, n, n to the Fungus Forest. Shoot the target-shaped mushroom
   three times, reloading between (shoot mushroom with bow / take arrow /
   put arrow in bow / shoot / put arrow in bow / shoot):
   >>> the third hit makes you an expert marksman                      [29]
   put arrow in bow (reload for later).

8. ICE WALL + WHITE DRAGON'S COAT
   s, s, w to the Frozen Foyer. break ice with pickaxe, then g four times:
   >>> the passage finally breaks through                              [32]
   s, se to the (dead) White Dragon's Lair. open claws:
   >>> take coat  -- the coat of many colours (a sundry item point)    [34]
   wear coat.

9. GYPSUM-FLOWER MAZE
   s to the Antechamber, then n, nw, n, e, e, e, e, ne to the Ebony Expanse; e
   into the maze. Five rooms: disturb a flower a DIFFERENT way each room and
   follow the direction its sediment blows. The fixed path is NE, S, W, N, E
   (the recorder reads the reported direction). Verbs used: break/push/pull/
   squeeze/cut.
   >>> stepping east out of the last room reaches the Obsidian Door    [36]

10. OBSIDIAN DOOR
   x door; unlock door with key (ebony key opens the first lock, revealing a
   second keyhole); unlock door with lockpick:
   >>> the obsidian door opens                                         [39]
   e into the Black Dragon's Lair (the door slams shut).

11. BLACK DRAGON — SURVIVE, SEVER, SUBDUE, ROB
   You have 13 free turns; he blasts acid on the 14th. Chat to burn turns
   (ask about black/white dragon, amylya, trinket, raif, red dragon, treasure,
   cousin, goblin; ask for ring; ask about ramp, plague, door), then on the 14th:
   >>> put lye in coat  -> the coat (now a base) neutralises the acid   [44]
   >>> kill dragon with sword  -> sever a claw (sword lost)             [46]
   >>> se to At the Top of the Ramp (a visit point)                    [48]
   >>> push boulder  -> boulders crush/subdue the dragon               [51]
   nw back down;
   >>> ask dragon for ring  -> he hands over the ring of heat resistance[53]
   ask dragon about door (he opens it); x ring; wear ring;
   >>> ask dragon about red dragon  -> he offers a secret ride          [58]

12. RIDE TO THE RED DRAGON
   sit on head. He carries you east; on his head you get a few turns per room
   (Antechamber: x paper/cube/statue), then z through Well-Lit Passage and Steep
   Cliff Face, x gate at the Red Dragon Gate, z (he speaks the gate open), z:
   >>> arrive at the Red Dragon's Lair (a visit point)                 [60]

13. THE DIVIDE — OUTLAST BOTH DRAGONS
   w to On The Divide (the safe middle). Wait (z x8). The black dragon's acid
   can't hurt you through the lye coat (it raises the water) and the red dragon's
   flames can't hurt you through the ring (they cost you some gear and rip the lye
   out of the coat):
   >>> the red dragon finally collapses                                [70]
   i (confirms you've lost firebug/moss/lye/lockpick/pickaxe), w to the Red
   Dragon's Plateau.

14. CHANDELIER + RIVER
   >>> shoot chandelier with bow  -> it drops onto the Red Dragon      [73]
   look (so you notice them); take ember; put ember in coat; n into the river.
   Float: z, z, z:
   >>> carried downstream past the red dragon                          [75]
   z, z, then:
   >>> remove ring  -> now the embered coat keeps you warm; you survive
       the freezing river and wash up on Pebble Beach                  [80]

15. THE JEWEL ROOM — TOUCH, DON'T TAKE
   n through the three Halls of the Ancients (x murals between the n moves) to the
   Jewel Room. x mirror, x pedestal, x jewel. Do NOT take the jewel (poison
   death).
   >>> touch mirror  -> pulled home through the mirror; you lie to the
       Druids that no Jewel exists and live a quiet life. WIN.         [90]
