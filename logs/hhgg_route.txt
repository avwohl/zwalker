HHGG (r60/861002) — verified 400-point route, in plain words
=============================================================
Point values in [brackets]; they sum to 400. ZIL citations in logs/hhgg_notes.md.

ACT 1 — EARTH (25)
  Wake in the dark: turn on light, get up, take + wear the gown, look in the
  pocket, swallow the analgesic [10 -> 10]. Take the screwdriver and toothbrush
  (endgame tools). South to the porch: take the junk mail (4th babel-fish
  blocker). South again and LIE IN FRONT OF THE BULLDOZER. Wait out the whole
  Ford/Prosser negotiation (6 waits) and only take the towel AFTER Prosser lies
  down in your place — taking it earlier makes Ford leave and strands you.
  South, west into the pub. Wait for Ford to buy the beer, then drink exactly
  three [3x5 -> 25]: fewer and the matter-transference beam kills you, and the
  fourth is refused. Buy the cheese sandwich but NEVER eat it (-30). Leave east;
  give the sandwich to the dog — the fed dog is what melts the war fleet's
  hearts four acts later. Wait for the Vogon demolition announcement; Ford drops
  the Sub-Etha Thumb at your feet: take device, press green button.

ACT 2 — VOGON SHIP (33 -> 85)
  The Dark: senses return after four wasted turns; the litany omits SMELL, so
  smell, then examine the (Ford-shaped) shadow to wake in the Vogon Hold
  [+8 -> 33]. Eat the peanuts at once (protein loss is lethal in ~6 turns).
  Babel fish Rube Goldberg — set all four blockers BEFORE pressing (only 5
  fish in the machine): remove gown, hang gown on hook, put towel on drain,
  wait for Ford to nap (he leaves the satchel and the Guide), take satchel,
  put satchel in front of panel, put mail on satchel, press dispenser button
  [+12 -> 45]. Re-wear the gown (the pocket fluff rides in it). Press the case
  switch: a recording names WHICH word of the poem's second verse opens the
  case (rolled per seed). Guards drag you to the poetry chairs: after the
  captain starts the first verse, ENJOY POETRY [+15 -> 60]; he then reads the
  second verse — note its FIRST line and pick the announced word (seed 1:
  "Fripping lyshus wimbgunts..." + third word = wimbgunts). Back in the hold,
  TYPE "WIMBGUNTS" — with the quotes; anything else blows up the case
  [+25 -> 85]. Take the plotter and the towel, wait through the airlock:
  spaced, and 29 seconds later the Heart of Gold scoops you up.

ACT 3 — HEART OF GOLD (110)
  Dark again (hearing missing): listen, then walk south — the "exit to port"
  is a lie. Entry Bay Two; one look and Ford walks you to the Bridge. Wait for
  the crew to head for the sauna. Drop the heavy gear (plotter, thumb,
  screwdriver, toothbrush, Guide) — the Bridge is the staging area. Down and
  aft: Eddie stonewalls the Engine Room five times — just keep going south
  (sure / absolutely / fake-out / reconsider / in). LOOK twice more and Eddie
  concedes: the spare Improbability Generator appears with pliers and rasp
  [+25 -> 110]. Take the drive, fetch a cup of Advanced Tea Substitute from the
  Nutrimat (touch pad, take cup — do NOT drink it, ever), then on the Bridge:
  drop drive and cup, put the small plug in the plotter, put the dangly bit in
  the cup. Push the switch for the first trip.

ACT 4 — FIVE IMPROBABILITY DREAMS (225; order is seed-dependent)
  Every trip starts in the Dark; after four turns the litany names the working
  sense, and the sense verb tells you where you are. Completed scenes bounce
  (instant dream deaths); each dream ends back in the Dark, hearing missing,
  listen / south, aboard again, up to the Bridge, push the switch.
  * LAIR (smell -> Beast-shaped shadow): say arthur dent; flee EAST; take a
    sharp stone; put towel on head (only works with the Beast in the room);
    carve my name on memorial — the Beast finds "Arthur Dent" on its list of
    victims and naps. Remove the towel, west, southwest: take the
    Nutrimat/Computer Interface off the skeleton [+25 -> 135]. Wait for the
    Beasthunters — their case of mistaken identity ends the dream and leaves
    the asteroid paint chipper in your hands.
  * PARTY, as Trillian (feel -> wine): drop plate, examine Arthur (that shows
    the jacket fluff), take fluff, open handbag, put fluff in handbag, take
    the plate back (or the hostess never leaves), hello phil — he ferries you
    off three turns later [+25].
  * SPEEDBOAT, as Zaphod (see -> orange sun of Damogran): look under seat
    (cushion fluff + key), take toolbox, steer boat toward spire — the
    autopilot blinks awake on the fourth crash-countdown turn and docks you.
    Stand, north to the Dais, wait for Trillian's stick-up, then: guards, drop
    the rifles; trillian, shoot the rifles; east into the ship [+25].
  * FORD ON EARTH (see -> yellow sun of Earth): open satchel, take fluff and
    towel, north, give towel to arthur ("...what's the word?" — idiot),
    go to prosser, prosser, lie down. Pub: buy peanuts, buy beer, drink beer
    [+15], drink beer (the house falls). East, north to the ruins, give fluff
    to arthur — he pockets it, which is why it is in YOUR gown afterwards.
    Wait for the fleet and the fumbled Thumb; Arthur presses the button.
  * WAR CHAMBER (hear, hum from BELOW): only reachable after "careless words" —
    a bungled 4+-word command after Earth's death (the route deliberately fires
    "put stone in satchel" on the Bridge) — and the route fires it ONLY once
    the other four dreams are done: a war-chamber re-roll after the particle
    would end in an unavoidable brain-materialisation death. In the chamber:
    take awl, wait ~13 turns — the fed dog defuses the Vl'Hurg/G'Gugvunt
    revenge, they beam you home microscopic, into your own synapse maze. Walk
    (40% of steps blocked) until the big black particle appears and take it
    the same turn [+25 -> 225]: it is your common sense ("Sense, Common for:
    Dent, Arthur"), and losing it is what later lets you hold no tea.

ACT 5 — TEA, MISSILES, WHALE (250)
  Back aboard: collect the cushion fluff and small key in the Hatchway (Zaphod's
  pockets emptied there). Galley: open panel, take board, put interface in
  nutrimat, touch pad — the Nutrimat sieges Eddie for the recipe for real tea.
  Bridge: put large plug in large receptacle and wait for the Magrathean
  missiles (launched when the tea job swamps the ship's circuits), then push
  the generator switch: improbability turns the missiles into a sperm whale
  (and a bowl of petunias), and the tea arrives in the Galley slot.
  Take tea, back to the Bridge, drop tea, remove dangly bit, put dangly bit in
  tea: with real tea as the Brownian source the next dark is STEERABLE. Drop
  everything except the Thing (worn gown and babel fish are exempt; anything
  else is lost with the whale). Push the switch; in the controlled dark, feel
  darkness each turn and taste the liquid only when it feels WARM (cold means
  the party): you wake licking the whale's stomach lining. Take flowerpot, put
  flowerpot in thing, and wait for the ground (the pot survives only inside the
  Thing).

ACT 6 — ENDGAME (400)
  Aboard again, the Thing (pot inside) finds its way back to you within a few
  turns — take it wherever it lands. On the Bridge: take out the pot, gather
  all four fluffs (pocket + satchel from the gown, jacket from the handbag —
  open it first, cushion from the floor), plant each in the pot, wait for the
  sprout, and carry the pot west into the sauna: it blooms [+25 -> 250]. Eat
  the fruit — the Tree of Foreknowledge shows WHICH tool Marvin will demand
  (seed 1: the toothbrush); that is why all ten tools must already be aboard.
  Fetch the named tool, then take tea and take no tea (possible now that your
  common sense is a maze ornament). Down, aft: OPEN DOOR — the screening door
  waves through anyone holding tea and no tea simultaneously. DRINK THE TEA
  [+100 -> 350] BEFORE stepping in: the Pantry's depression is lethal below
  300 points, survivable at 350 [+25 -> 375]. Say marvin, open the hatch (the
  ship has landed by now), then east, down to the Hatchway, drop everything
  but the tool (the Access Space admits one carried item), east, and wait the
  twelve turns. Marvin shambles in, asks for the tool, give toothbrush to
  marvin [+25 -> 400]; he unjams the mechanism in three-tenths of a second.
  West, down, and one single foot on the ancient dust of Magrathea ends the
  game at 400/400.
