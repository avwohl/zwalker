BALLYHOO — verified route to 200/200 (20 awards x 10 points).
Written from my own playthrough; ZIL cites live in logs/ballyhoo_notes.md.
Award order below is the order the points are actually scored on this route.

  1. Cross the tightrope with the balance pole ........................  10
  2. Greet Harry with helium in your lungs (pass the turnstile) .......  20
  3. Knock on Clown Alley wearing the clown mask (fool Chuckles) ......  30
  4. Walk through the canvas pleats into the Prop Tent (first time) ...  40
  5. Find the circus ticket in the bleacher garbage ..................  50
  6. Fish the skeleton keys off the cage wall with the pole ..........  60
  7. Find the cigarette case under the lion stand ....................  70
  8. Show/give the cigarette case to Jenny (she recalls Andrew) ......  80
  9. Finish the hypnosis dream as the granola bar falls ..............  90
 10. Get the transistor radio from Tina (shake hands) ...............  100
 11. Soothe Mahler with recorded classical music ...................  110
 12. Catch a live mouse in the bucket ..............................  120
 13. Get rid of Hannibal the elephant (show him the mouse) .........  130
 14. Gain access into the white wagon office ......................  140

Narrative walkthrough
=====================

OPENING (no points, but three flags are set here that pay off later).
  From In the Wings go south to the Connection and HELP THUMB while the
  midget is struggling to drink -- this sets HELPED-THUMB, which later keeps
  Thumb from ratting you out in Clown Alley, hides him under the blackjack
  table, and lets him hoist Chelsea. West for the clown MASK, south to the
  Back Yard, west into the Prop Tent, EXAMINE then HIDE BEHIND TAFT (a
  cardboard cutout) and wait out the Munrab/detective conversation: you learn
  Chelsea has been kidnapped and the ransom note is "not an inside job."

AWARD 1 (tightrope, +10) and the balloon.
  East x2 to Near White Wagon, TAKE POLE. North x3 to the Performance Ring,
  drop the mask, UP to the platform, and walk EAST x6 across the tightrope --
  the fiberglass pole is what keeps you balanced. TAKE the helium BALLOON on
  the far platform, west x6 back, down x2, TAKE ALL.

AWARD 2 (helium/Harry, +10).
  South x2, west, south to the Back Yard. UNTIE BALLOON, INHALE HELIUM, then
  immediately HARRY, HELLO -- your squeaky voice fools the blind guard Harry
  and the turnstile buzzes open. South (through the turnstile) and west.

AWARD 3 (clown mask, +10) and the newsprint scrap.
  At Camp West, WEAR MASK and KNOCK ON DOOR: Chuckles mistakes you for
  "Malcom" and lets you into Clown Alley. CLOSE DOOR, SEARCH ASHES (a scrap of
  newsprint surfaces), TAKE SCRAP, then one WAIT -- at clown-counter 6 with
  the door shut he blurts the Annie Oakley tip (setting the sideshow-front
  gate to 7) and throws you out unmasked.

AWARD 4 (canvas pleats, +10).
  GO UNDER THE WALL to slip through the pleated canvas into the Prop Tent
  (this also drops a mousetrap disguised as a bit of wood, for later).

AWARD 5 (ticket, +10).
  East, north, east, north, northeast to Under the Bleachers. SEARCH GARBAGE,
  TAKE TICKET, PUNCH BLUE DOT (you play the rest of the game as a man).

AWARD 6 (keys, +10).
  Southwest, south to the Connection, PUT TICKET IN SLOT, east through the
  turnstile, south, southeast to the Menagerie Nook. LOOK IN CAGE, TAKE KEYS
  WITH POLE (fish the hoop ring off the wall), UNLOCK/OPEN DOOR, north into
  the cage, TAKE BUCKET (the meat rides inside) and HEADPHONES.

THE WHIP and the STOOL (setup).
  Loop out to Camp East (south, nw, north, west x2, south, west, south, east),
  UNLOCK/OPEN the baggage COMPARTMENT, TAKE WHIP. Back to the Connection
  (north, east, north), PUT TICKET IN SLOT, east x3 to the Far End of Midway,
  north, northeast to the Fat Lady's east half, TAKE STOOL.

AWARD 7 (cigarette case, +10).
  Northwest, south, west x3, north x2 to the Performance Ring, UNLOCK/OPEN the
  round CAGE, west to the Lions' Den. WHIP SMOOTH LION x3 (the smooth/maneless
  one) until it lies down, LIFT GRATE, THROW MEAT IN PASSAGE, step east then
  west, LOWER GRATE (the lions are gone), SEARCH STAND -> cigarette case.

AWARD 8 (Jenny, +10).
  East, south x3, west to the Back Yard, GIVE CASE TO HARRY (he says it's
  Andrew's). North, east, PUT TICKET IN SLOT, east x2, south to Jennifer's
  Boudoir. SHOW CASE TO JENNY then GIVE CASE TO JENNY: Jenny remembers
  Andrew's part in the plot and chases him off, naming Katz's trailer.

AWARD 9 (hypnosis dream, +10).
  North x2 to the Hypnotist's Parlor. GIVE TICKET TO RIMSHAW, then RIMSHAW,
  HYPNOTIZE ME -- you relive being stuck in the Standing-Room-Only seats.
  WAIT x4 until the hawker appears, BUY CANDY FROM HAWKER, GIVE $1.85 TO
  HAWKER for the granola. Thread the seat grid out (east, up, east, down,
  east, up, east, down, south) to the concession line, work the long/short
  line con to grab the $2.25 frozen banana, BITE BANANA and DROP BANANA to
  shed the monkey. North, ASK HAWKER ABOUT CANDY (your $12.81 dream-tab is
  exactly right), thread back to your seat -- the granola falls and you wake.

AWARD 10 (radio, +10).
  South, west, GO UNDER THE WALL, SEARCH GARBAGE, TAKE GRANOLA BAR. South,
  east x2, north, northeast to the Fat Lady, SHOW GRANOLA BAR TO TINA, TINA
  HELLO, SHAKE HANDS. Northwest, TAKE RADIO.

AWARD 11 (Mahler classical, +10).
  Carry only the radio and headphones to the Menagerie Nook and up to the Top
  of Cage. Play the built-in cassette to hear Rimshaw's subliminal, then
  SET RADIO TO 1170 (classical) and RECORD over tape positions 0-6. Rewind,
  climb DOWN (the harmless "you fall / you have died" gag -- see the notes),
  TAKE ALL, enter Mahler's cage, PLAY TAPE: the ape snatches the classical
  headphones and is soothed. SEARCH STRAW, OPEN TRAP DOOR, TAKE RIBBON, out,
  lock the cage.

AWARD 12 (mouse, +10).
  To the Prop Tent: TOUCH WOOD WITH POLE (springs the trap safely -- a bare
  hand is fatal), TAKE/DROP the sprung trap with its cheese, then step out and
  back twice so the field mouse comes to feed. CATCH MOUSE WITH BUCKET,
  TAKE MOUSE.

AWARD 13 (Hannibal, +10).
  Ticket through the turnstile to the Menagerie, SHOW MOUSE TO ELEPHANT x2 and
  WAIT: terrified Hannibal shears his chain and stampedes through the fence.

AWARD 14 (white wagon, +10).
  Southwest to the (now reachable) Near White Wagon, drop your load, UP,
  TURN CRANK to open the roof panel, LOOK IN WAGON, KNOCK ON DOOR to lure
  Munrab out, then IN through the panel into the Office. LOCK DOOR, SEARCH
  DESK, TAKE SPREADSHEET (Eddie Smaldone's shrinking salary), MOVE DESK under
  the panel, UP and out. Read the sheet; back down, TAKE ALL, west, ASK HARRY
  ABOUT EDDIE -> Eddie is Chuckles.

AWARD 15 (Blue Room, +10).
  East, northeast, southeast to the Menagerie Nook, SLIDE TICKET UNDER FRONT
  (the clown-counter-7 gate opens the sideshow panel), east into the Blue
  Room, TAKE TICKET. OPEN PANEL forces a $1 blackjack hand during which Thumb,
  hidden below, taps the dealer's hole card on your foot (stand immediately).
  OPEN PANEL again, west out. At the Connection Thumb reappears and points you
  back (marking a "new dealer"); slide the ticket a second time, LOOK UNDER
  TABLE, TAKE SUITCASE -- Chelsea's screaming keister -- and the thugs wrestle
  it away and bolt up onto the tent.

AWARD 16 (tent prod, +10).
  Drop your load, UP x2 to On the Tent. Dodge the electric prod (WAIT, walk
  around with EAST to keep your footing), and when it lingers TAKE SHAFT then
  PULL SHAFT: your unseen opponent loses the prod and crashes down.

THE DRUNK DETECTIVE (setup).
  Down x2 (this descent just "clambers" -- the death gag already fired above).
  Recover your gear (leaving key/whip/stool), FILL BUCKET WITH WATER at the
  fountain, POUR WATER ON DETECTIVE, ASK DETECTIVE ABOUT CHELSEA -> he coughs
  up the ransom NOTE and the trade CARD.

AWARD 17 (scare Chuckles, +10).
  East, south, UP to the Wardrobe Closet, TAKE ALL (dress/suit and
  shawl/jacket combos). Loop to Camp East where Chuckles lurks. EDDIE, HELLO
  to name him, then SHOW RIBBON / SCRAP / NOTE / SPREADSHEET / CARD TO EDDIE:
  the fifth clue breaks him and he flees.

AWARD 18 (Chelsea, +10).
  SEARCH POCKET for the VEIL; WEAR VEIL, DRESS, JACKET to pass as a woman.
  KNOCK ON DOOR -- Katzenjammer, fooled, steps out. East inside, CLOSE DOOR,
  TAKE CROWBAR, MOVE MOOSE to reveal the crawl-space hole, open the door.
  West x2, OPEN TRAILER DOOR WITH CROWBAR into Clown Alley, TAKE THUMB. Back
  north/east/east, PUT THUMB IN HOLE, WAIT while he boosts the girl up, then
  TAKE CHELSEA.

AWARD 19 (net, +10).
  Carry Chelsea out (west, north, east, northeast, north, west, north x2):
  Munrab bolts, the ape grabs the girl onto the platform, and the endgame
  begins. CLAP to fire the roustabout's post-hypnotic trigger, then
  ROUSTABOUT, GET NET.

AWARD 20 (Mahler drops Chelsea, +10 -> WIN).
  Strip the disguise, drop everything, fetch the LION STAND from the den as a
  step, TAKE RADIO, CLIMB STAND, UP onto the platform (net held, so the
  spooked ape is safe among the guy wires). UP again flushes Mahler to the far
  platform. TURN ON RADIO (already tuned to 1170) and cross EAST partway until
  the WPDL "Pledge Week" break interrupts the music, then turn around. Down to
  the Office, CALL WPDL to pledge so the station will finally play Vivaldi.
  Back up onto the platform, TAKE RADIO, and cross EAST x5: the classical
  music becalms Mahler, who drops Chelsea into the net (+10 = 200/200). You
  catch the wire, WAIT x3, and are flung safely into the net -- game won,
  "having saved The Traveling Circus That Time Forgot, Inc."
