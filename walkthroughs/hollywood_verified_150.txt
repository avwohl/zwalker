# RANDOM prop from the pool each turn his hands are empty

# take the prop he holds; under seed 1 he grabs bag/sword/clippers, so
# club/stick/mop are free for the three hits (any three hits fell him,
# elevator.zil:1038-1082). Everything else is deterministic clockwork:
# both safe combinations are constants (wall safe R3-L7-R5,

# the Atomic Chihuahua model, tanks, planes and rocket are turn-counted

# bucket drips 1 unit/turn (I-DRIP, elevator.zil:556-597), the rope burns

# maze is a static byte table (HM-TABLE, hedgemaze.zil:1266+), and the
